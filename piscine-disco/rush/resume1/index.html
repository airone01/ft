About Me

Who I Am

I am Ariane Yeong, a former Penrose pilot bound by fate and the echoes of memory. My journey began on Rotfront, where isolation and artistic ambition shaped my existence before duty called me to the stars.

Trained as a radio officer, I was selected for the Penrose Program, a voyage into the unknown meant to seek life beyond the stars. Aboard the Penrose-512, I formed an unbreakable bond with Elster, defying the program's cold regulations.

Through cycles uncountable, I faced the slow decay of my vessel and my own form, trapped between waking and dreaming. Now, I exist as a whisper in the corridors of time, my presence stretching beyond the limits of mortality.
